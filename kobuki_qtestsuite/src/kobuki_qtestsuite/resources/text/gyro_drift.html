UP5 - Gyro Drift

Overview

Estimates the gyro drift by checking against a turtlebot mounted kinect (fake laser) that is pointed at a wall

Usage

  • Place the robot in front of a wall.
  • Configure the rotational speed.
  • Press start.
  • Scan error angle (gyro vs laser) is plotted on the graph.

Tips

You might also want to open up rqt_gui with another plot window to view live the graphs of laser_scan_angle.scan_angle and gyro_scan_angle.scan_angle.
